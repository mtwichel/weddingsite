Our Story

Our story began in the fall of 2016. As freshmen in college, we both decided to join the debate team after previously participating in high school debate. We joined the debate team for Rocky Mountain College in Montana and began a slow friendship. Throughout the year we grew into a deeper friendship through shared experiences at debate meets and hardships in school. Eventually, one night, Marcus asked Margaret out on a date and she agreed.

The next few months were wonderful as we grew closer, spent more time together, and went on a debate trip to New York. We got to spend the summer together but all too soon fall came and with it, both of us transferring to schools on opposite sides of the country. Long distance was hard but we stuck it out, always looking forward to school breaks when we could be together again.

It's been a long three years and they've been filled with a lot of struggles but they've also been filled with shared laughter over podcasts and television shows we both share, wonderful summer months that we wished would never end, and hope that someday we would be able to truly begin our lives together. Now, as that day approaches, we are so thankful for the love and support we've received from family and friends, and we are so excited to share this wonderful day with all of you.
